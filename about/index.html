About Us

Metallurgy collaboration is an associate company of Ashoka Group Incorporated in 1978

We supply critical machined components like

  • Cylinder Heads
  • Blocks
  • Two-Wheeler Components
  • Hermetic Compressor Parts
  • Hydraulic Components

We are benefited by state-of-Art manufacturing facility, equipped with best in class shell core machines and highly qualified and technically experienced team.

Founded on the core values of Excellence, Passion and Integrity, Metallurgy collaboration, is a leading manufacturer and supplier of Engineering Components and Machined Sub-Assemblies.

What We Do

We are one of the leading manufacturer of shell Mould & Green Sand Castings in Grey cast Iron , Ductile Iron & SIMO Graded as per the Customer Specifications

Component ranging from 0.30 to 25 Kgs with minimum Wall Thickness of 2.0 mm

We accomplish this with a diverse range of machinery, namely...

  • Induction Furnace of 550 kW – 500 Kg. with melt rate of 1 MT per Hour.
  • Fully mechanized mould handling system with monorail pouring system.
  • Shot Blasting Machine – (2 Nos) –300 Kgs per 5 minutes–Conveyor Belt Type -Twin Turbine - Make Surfex
  • Grinder , Chippers , Pneumatic grinder, Sand Blasting –for cleaning of casting internal cavity

We specialize in Cylinder Head required for Diesel Engines, Tractors, Light Commercial Vehicles.
